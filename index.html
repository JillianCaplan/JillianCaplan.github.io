A Bit About Me

I am a senior undergraduate student in honours Psychology (Behavioural Neuroscience) with a minor in Multidisciplinary Studies in Science at Concordia University. I am the Co-Founder of Concordia’s Journal of Psychology and Neuroscience and currently serve as the Co-Editor-in-Chief. I hope to pursue doctoral studies as a scientist-practitioner, where I can investigate the mechanisms of neurocognition and gene expression to better understand their role in psychiatric illnesses.

I am extremely passionate about student mental health and wellbeing. I proudly founded the Stronger than Stigma chapter at Concordia University and I am the Chief Operating Officer at WellMTL. I love to learn new skills (like coding my own website) and in my free time, I enjoy yoga.
